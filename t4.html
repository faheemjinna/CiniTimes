RRR Shoot Postponed :

We all know how much the pandemic situation has caused a wide spread damage to the country both the health and wealth of people and nation. Worldwide lakhs of people are being rested and the test reveals to be positive symptom for millions of people. Here in our country most of the people are found to be tested as positive, the government is in full swing of vaccination drive and as for now people who are aged 18 and above are asked to get vaccinated. The dangerous virus is infecting more in the cinema friends and even politicians, as of now Tollywood fame Junior NTR is found to be Corona Positive. Junior NTR is currently was in shooting for S.S. Rajamouli’s film RRR, but due to this pandemic situation of COVID, the film shooting has been currently closed and has been postponed. The film titled PAN India and is yet to be released worldwide on 13th OCT 2021. PENN Films got the rights for the film and it is to be released at the celebration of Dussehra.

Celebrities tweets on health of Junior NTR :

Junior NTR as known to be infected, he announced this news over the social media platform Twitter, as his followers, wellwishers, familymenbers, cinema frirends and politicians are tweeting back to him to recover quickly. Recently Tollywood Superstar Mahesh Babu also tweeted about his welfare, Mahesh Babu tweeted as “ Get Well Soon Brother, Strength and Prayers be carefull “. As a matter of fact, he got many tweets even from politicians also, Chandrababu Naidu tweeted him “ Recover Quickly “ by the other hand Nara Lokesh tweeted him as “ He wants to recover from corona quickly “. As the film RRR was been shot under the direction of S.S. Rajamouli, his next film is decided to work under the direction of Koratala Shiva.
