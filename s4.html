Upendra Decides to Buy & Sell Crops from Unsettled Farmers

Upendra decides to buy crops of farmers who are unsettled and distributing them to those who need them and who have not grown their crops due to Lockdown.
Cinitimes
Scroll Down To Discover
Font size:

Celebrities are willing to help the infected peoples :

Sandalwood Industry is been infected with New Mutant Strain The Second wave of Corona, as the highest number of Covid cases are being reported in the State of Karnataka, especially at the city of Bangalore the widespread of Covid has found and most of the industries are running under loss, and the Indian film industry is also one among them. As the cases are increasing rapidly, most of the celebrities are willing to help the infected peoples through different types of contribution, Actor Upendra is one among them. He has now decided to Purchase and Distribute the Crops from the unsettled Farmers.

Upendra Decides to Buy & Sell Crops :

Real Star Upendra has helped many Cine Artist Families during Hardships and has decided to Purchase and Distribute the crops from those who have been affected due to Lockdown. He is as of now buying crops of farmers who are unsettled and distributing them for those who need it, and who have not grown their crops due to the second wave of corona and lockdown.“Farmers are in dire straits when they cannot sell the crop grown because of the Corona lockdown. With the donor’s money, we buy vegetables directly from farmers and distribute them with groceries, ”Upendra tweeted.
