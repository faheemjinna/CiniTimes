Pathu Thala Movie will resume – Rahman Starts Composing

Pathu Thala Movie shoot will resume, this film will be marked as a third multi-starer movie for Silambarasan, as he had done “Vaanam & Chekka Chivandha Vaanam”.

Scroll Down To Discover
Font size:

Pathu Thala Movie Cast :

The film “Pathu Thala” is an Action Thrilled Multi Starrer Tamil movie which is the remake of Blockbuster Kannada Movie “Mufti”. The film starring Actor Silambarasan who plays the role of a Gangster, followed by Gautham Karthik Son of Navarasa Nayagan Karthik plays the role of a Police Officer. Kalaiarasan (Adhe Kangal, Madras Fame) plays a pivotal role in this film, Actress Priya Bhavani Shankar who will be seen as the Lead Actress, followed by Teejay Arunasalam (Asuran Fame) who played the role of Son of Dhanush in the Movie “Asuran” will now be seen in another pivotal role in this film. As the movie “Mufti” Kannada Version which has been Directed by Narthan, is now Directed by Obeli. N. Krishna (Sillunu Oru Kadhal Fame), the film is produced under the banner of Studio Green Gnanavel Raja, and the music is composed by Isai Puyal A.R. Rahman.

Rahman Starts Composing for Pathu Thala :

In a recent interview through call, Krishna stated that “ I’m teaming up with Rahman sir just as Gnanavel sir after Sillunu Oru Kaadhal. Watch out for a heartfelt number in the film. I advised him obviously we do and how could there not be one heartfelt melody? ”. A.R. Rahman started his work on composing the music for the movie and we are ready to hear his heartfelt melody once again after the Vinnaithandi Varuvaya movie. Due to this pandemic situation of Covid-19 Lockdown, all the film industry shoots have been postponed, the shoot of Pathu Thala will resume only after all the restrictions are cleared, and the situation of Corona after gradually reducing the daily counts. This film will be marked as the third multi-starer movie for Silambarasan, as he had done “Vaanam & Chekka Chivandha Vaanam”.
