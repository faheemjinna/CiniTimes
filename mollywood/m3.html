About Divya Unni :

Divya Unni is a Malayalam movie Actress, born in Cochin, Kerala, she has likewise done numerous Tamil movies and one movie in Telugu, her first introduction as a Child Artist in a Malayalam film “Neeyethra Dhanya” which is directed by Jersey a Malayalam movie director and her first debut as a lead actress in the film “Kalyana Sowkandhikkam” which is directed by Director Vinayan. She is likewise a Classical Dancer, performs and teaches different types of dance like Bharathanatiyam, Mohiniaatam, and Kuchipudi. She performed at many dance festivals in India, she got Aravindaksha Memorial Award, the Abhinaya Tilaka Puraskaram for the best state dance execution. It is supposed to be that, she is the cousin sister of Remya Nambeesan and Meera Nandhan Malayalam actresses.

Divya Unni’s School of Dance :

Divya Unni has moved to United States, Houston, Texas with her family and settled there for quite a while previously, chasing after advancing Indian culture she is fostering the gifts of Young Children in the United States close to where she resides right now. She is now the director of Sreepadam School of Arts where she shows dance for Young children.Divya Unni is currently dynamic on Instagram and been posting numerous posts which are going on there at her Dance School just as her own experiences. As of now, she posted her exhibition performed during the auspicious day on Sankara Jayanthi. She posted on Instagram as “Basking in that Divine energy. Getting ready for my morning classes. Adi Shankaracharya and Devi. Sharing one of my favorite sanchari on this auspicious day of Shankarajayanthi, A snippet from the Amma Anandadayini varnam – a composition in Gambheeranattai by the legendary Sri Balamuralikrishna. Hope you all enjoy it. Video courtesy: soorya festival video archives 2018. The full video is available on the Soorya India festival YouTube Channel”.
